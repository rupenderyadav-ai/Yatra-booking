Day by Day

The full route, mapped out

DAY 0

Arrival — Dehradun

Arrive in Dehradun and transfer to the hotel. Evening briefing covers the journey flow, safety instructions, and baggage allocation. Overnight stay in Dehradun.

DAY 1

Dehradun → Kedarnath

Morning helicopter departure from Sahastradhara Helipad to Sitapur, followed by a shuttle flight to Kedarnath Helipad. After VIP darshan at Kedarnath Temple, guests proceed to their hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

DAY 2

Kedarnath → Badrinath

Morning flight from Sitapur to Badrinath. Check in at the hotel and proceed for darshan at Badrinath Temple. Evening visit to Mana Village (optional). Overnight stay in Badrinath.

DAY 3

Badrinath → Dehradun

Early morning darshan and optional Abhishek at Badrinath Temple. After breakfast, fly back to Dehradun. Transfer to airport or railway station for onward journey.

DAY 0

Arrival — Dehradun

Arrive in Dehradun and transfer to the hotel. Evening briefing covers the journey flow, safety instructions, and baggage allocation. Overnight stay in Dehradun.

DAY 1

Dehradun → Yamunotri

Early morning helicopter flight to Kharsali. From here, proceed to Yamunotri Temple via palki or pony for darshan. Return to Kharsali by evening for overnight stay.

DAY 2

Yamunotri → Gangotri

Fly from Kharsali to Harsil. Drive onward to Gangotri Temple for darshan. Afternoon at leisure in Harsil, with time to explore the village. Overnight stay in Harsil.

DAY 3

Gangotri → Kedarnath

Helicopter flight from Harsil to Guptkashi, followed by a shuttle flight to Kedarnath. Short walk to the temple for darshan. Return to Guptkashi for overnight stay.

DAY 4

Kedarnath → Badrinath

Morning flight from Guptkashi to Badrinath. Check in at the hotel and proceed for darshan at Badrinath Temple. Evening visit to Mana Village (optional). Overnight stay in Badrinath.

DAY 5

Badrinath → Dehradun

Early morning darshan and optional Abhishek at Badrinath Temple. After breakfast, fly back to Dehradun. Transfer to airport or railway station for onward journey.
